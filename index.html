Nazamly Services

Event making

Nazamly works to organize the events from A to Z, facilitate the reservation of the hall, make special designs for the event, provide the necessary acoustics and lighting, and work to provide full coverage in terms of photography and making montage videos for the event and has individuals trained to receive and prepare the event well.

Event organization

Organize a team that specializes in providing trained personnel to organize events anywhere in Upper Egypt so that it can come out well and in an organized manner.

Course organization

The team organizes the halls for the courses from the entry and exit process for the trainees and providing various required tools and amenities, as it works to provide the halls if you do not have a room for training and advertising on the social networking pages to attract the trainees.

Graduation party organization

The team works to organize graduation parties from A to Z, including graduation uniforms, certificates and shields for graduates, as well as reserving halls, providing the organizational process and memorial photos for students, and providing special paragraphs for the ceremony.



Who is this?
